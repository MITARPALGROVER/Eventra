Frequently Asked Questions

How does the rental process work?

Browse our catalog, select the items you need, choose your rental dates, and complete your booking. We'll confirm availability and handle delivery and setup at your event location on the agreed date.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 7+ days before the event receive a full refund. Cancellations 3-6 days before receive a 50% refund. Cancellations less than 72 hours before the event are non-refundable.

Do you offer delivery and setup services?

Yes, we offer delivery and professional setup for all orders. Delivery fees depend on your location and the size of your order. Setup and teardown services are included for larger orders.

How do I become a vendor on Eventra?

Click on "Become a Vendor" and complete our application form. Our team will review your submission and contact you within 2-3 business days to discuss the next steps and onboarding process.

What happens if equipment is damaged?

All rentals include standard insurance. Minor wear and tear is expected, but significant damage will be assessed after the event. Customers are responsible for costs beyond normal wear and tear.
